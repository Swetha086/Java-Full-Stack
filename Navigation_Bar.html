Biryani

Chicken Biryani is a flavorful and aromatic dish made with marinated chicken, fragrant basmati rice, and a blend of spices. The chicken is typically cooked with yogurt and a variety of spices such as cumin, coriander, turmeric, and garam masala, which infuse the meat with rich flavors. The rice is often cooked separately and then layered with the chicken before being steamed together to allow the flavors to meld. Biryani is often garnished with fried onions, fresh herbs, and sometimes boiled eggs or nuts for added texture and flavor. It is a popular dish in South Asian cuisine and is enjoyed by many around the world.
